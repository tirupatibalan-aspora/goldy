## What is Goldy?

Goldy is not here to replace your project manager. Goldy is the **intelligent hand beside them** — translating the speed of your vibe into a language the entire feature team, stakeholders, and leadership can understand.

We're entering an era where intelligence moves faster than process. Codebases grow overnight. Designs ship before specs are finalized. Solo developers build what entire teams used to. The gap isn't in talent — it's in visibility. Your PM knows the roadmap. Your stakeholders know the business goals. But nobody has real-time clarity on where the code actually is, what's blocking it, and what needs to happen in the next 48 hours to hit the deadline.

That's Goldy. A bridge between the developer's reality and the team's expectations. Not a replacement for human judgment — a force multiplier for it.

**The world Goldy lives in:**

Product development is no longer a waterfall. It's not even agile anymore — it's vibes. A solo dev pushes 54 Kotlin files on Monday, gets Figma feedback on Tuesday, rewrites the MVI layer on Wednesday, and ships a PR on Thursday that touches 12 modules. The PM asks "where are we?" and the honest answer is: "everywhere and nowhere — let me check git."

Goldy checks git for you. Checks Figma. Checks Slack. And every morning, your PM, your stakeholders, your entire feature team gets a visual dashboard that tells the real story — with progress bars that come from actual commits, not optimistic estimates.

## The Bigger Picture

Goldy isn't just a reporting tool. It's preparation for what's coming.

The teams that will thrive in this era of accelerating intelligence aren't the ones with the most process. They're the ones with the most clarity. When AI can write code, review designs, and generate tests — the bottleneck moves to **coordination, context, and communication**. That's the hard problem. That's what Goldy solves.

Think of it this way: today Goldy reads your GitHub and produces a status report. Tomorrow, Goldy understands your PRD, watches your Figma evolve, tracks your test coverage trend, knows which architectural decisions were made in Slack three weeks ago, and synthesizes all of it into a living document that keeps your entire team — from the intern to the CTO — on the same page.

This is the beginning of a larger, deeper product development journey. Goldy grows with your ambition.

## Who is Goldy For?

Goldy serves the entire product development chain:

- **Project Managers** — Goldy is your extra pair of eyes on the codebase. You focus on roadmap and people; Goldy gives you the technical ground truth every morning. No more chasing developers for updates.
- **Stakeholders & Leadership** — One link. One dashboard. Real numbers from real code. Share it in your Monday standup, your board meeting, your investor update. The data is always fresh.
- **Solo Developers & Small Teams** — You're building iOS + Android with 2 people (or alone). Goldy handles the visibility so you can stay in flow. Your PM gets the update before they even ask.
- **Feature Teams** — When 3-4 people work across platforms, designs, and backend — Goldy keeps everyone aligned on what's done, what's blocked, and what to prioritize next.
- **Remote & Async Teams** — Replace daily standups with a Goldy dashboard link. Everyone reads it on their own schedule. The conversation starts at "what should we do about this risk?" instead of "so what did you work on yesterday?"

## The Goldy Philosophy

```
Intelligence is moving fast.
Your team deserves to move with it.
```

Goldy believes:

1. **Clarity is a competitive advantage** — The team that sees clearest, ships fastest
2. **AI should augment, not replace** — Goldy doesn't make decisions. Goldy makes sure the right people have the right information to make great decisions
3. **Reports should be beautiful** — Because when stakeholders can actually read the status, they stop interrupting developers to ask for it
4. **The speed of vibe is real** — Modern development moves at the speed of intuition, iteration, and intelligence. Traditional project tracking can't keep up. Goldy can.
5. **Preparation beats process** — Goldy doesn't impose ceremonies. It prepares your team for even larger, more complex product journeys by building the muscle of real-time awareness now
6. **Every team member deserves context** — From the intern to the CTO, everyone should know where the product stands. Goldy democratizes project visibility.

## Setup

### Prerequisites

- GitHub repository with your code
- Slack workspace with a status channel
- Figma file with your designs (optional)
- Claude Desktop with Cowork mode (for scheduled reports)

### Quick Start

1. **Clone this repo**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/tirupatibalan-aspora/goldy.git
   ```

2. **Enable GitHub Pages**
   - Go to repo Settings → Pages
   - Source: Deploy from a branch
   - Branch: `main` → `/docs`
   - Save

3. **Your dashboard is live at:**
   ```
   https://tirupatibalan-aspora.github.io/goldy/
   ```

4. **Set up the scheduled task** in Claude Desktop / Cowork:
   - Goldy runs daily at 9 AM
   - Analyzes your Git repos, Figma designs, Slack threads
   - Generates the HTML dashboard
   - Posts the link to your Slack channel

### How the Memory System Works

Goldy uses a two-tier memory system to stay context-aware across sessions:

**Tier 1 — Hot Cache (`CLAUDE.md`)**
~80 lines of essential context loaded every session. Who you are, your team, active projects, tech stack, current branches, preferences. Enough for Goldy to be useful immediately.

**Tier 2 — Deep Memory (`memory/`)**
Full knowledge base loaded on-demand. Architecture details, product decisions, test gap analysis, company integrations, historical context. Only pulled when needed — keeps sessions fast and token-efficient.

This means Goldy remembers your project across sessions — architecture decisions, team preferences, product answers, open questions, past risks — without burning context re-reading everything. It's how Goldy stays intelligent over time, not just in the moment.
